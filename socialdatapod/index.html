Personal online datastore for social data. Built on the SOLID Protocol, advocated by Tim Berners-Lee, inventor of the World Wide Web.

User-Centric

You should own and control your data, not apps, AIs, and algorithms of profit-oriented companies.

SOLID Protocol

Built on Tim Berners-Lee's vision for a decentralized web where users control their personal data.

AI-Enhanced

Powerful tools for developers to make social data more useful and explore new AI-powered use cases.

"When I invented the World Wide Web, I envisioned technology that would empower people and enable collaboration. Somewhere along the way, we lost that human-first approach; today’s web often prioritises profits over people. Solid returns the web to its roots by giving everyone direct control over their own data."
— Tim Berners-Lee, Creator of the World Wide Web

Social Datapod assists in the transition to this user-centric data model, focusing primarily on X data while remaining open to contributors building parallels for other social platforms.
